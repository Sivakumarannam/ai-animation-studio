# OBJECTIVE

Implement **Phase 4 — RAG & Knowledge Intelligence Engine**.

This phase builds the long-term knowledge layer that powers every future AI feature.

The Knowledge Engine must:

* Reduce hallucinations
* Maintain long-term memory
* Retrieve relevant context automatically
* Support semantic search
* Store embeddings

* Improve Story Intelligence
